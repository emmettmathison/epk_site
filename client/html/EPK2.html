TAU DOLLY is a composer from Los Angeles. His work explores pop forms and lifeforms. He is interested in the potential of collective sound-making to democratize sonic group-mind, or:
autonomous, sonorous, superconsciousness, or:
let’s all hold hands and scream as loud as we can.
Music Is For Everyone, Abolish Death, No Cops On Mars etc...
